Bench Press

The Bench Press is a weightlifting exercise performed on a flat bench, typically using a barbell or dumbbells. It primarily targets the muscles of the upper body, including the chest (pectoralis major), shoulders (deltoids), and triceps. It also engages the muscles of the back, core, and arms to a lesser extent.

Lat Pulldown

The Lat Pulldown is a back exercise performed on a cable machine. By pulling a wide-grip bar down towards the chest, it targets the lats and upper back muscles. It engages the shoulders, arms, and upper body, making it an effective exercise for overall upper body strength and development.
